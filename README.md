# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Using Retail Sales Data

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Python** and **Microsoft Power BI**. The objective is to analyze retail sales data, identify business trends, and provide actionable insights through interactive visualizations.

The project follows the complete data analytics workflow, including data collection, cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and dashboard creation.

**Business Question:**
**"How can retail sales data be analyzed to identify profitable products, high-performing regions, and sales trends for better business decisions?"**

---

## 🚀 What This Project Does

* Cleans and preprocesses retail sales data using Python.
* Performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
* Identifies sales trends across regions and product categories.
* Analyzes the relationship between sales, profit, and quantity.
* Creates an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Provides business insights and recommendations.

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Sales are positively skewed, with a few high-value orders contributing significantly to total revenue.
* Some regions consistently outperform others in total sales.
* Certain product categories generate higher revenue and profit.
* Sales and profit have a positive relationship, although higher sales do not always guarantee higher profit.
* Quantity sold has a direct impact on total sales.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory for high-demand products.
* Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing regions.
* Review pricing strategies for products with low profit margins.
* Promote underperforming product categories.
* Use monthly sales trends for better inventory and demand planning.
